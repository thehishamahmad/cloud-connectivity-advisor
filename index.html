Overview

This advisor helps organizations identify the most practical connectivity approach for cloud environments. It is designed to avoid over-engineering by first determining whether the organization truly requires network-level hybrid connectivity, or whether simpler secure access through IAP is sufficient.

Why This Matters

Avoid Over-Engineering

Not every cloud environment needs VPN or Interconnect. Some workloads only require secure administrative access using IAP.

Control Connectivity Cost

Connectivity design should match actual traffic, resiliency, and business requirements instead of defaulting to expensive architecture.

Support Hybrid Workloads

For workloads that require continuous private connectivity, the advisor helps distinguish between Cloud VPN, HA VPN, and Interconnect.

Plan for Data Movement

Cloud-to-on-premise download requirements can directly affect bandwidth, performance, cost, and connectivity strategy.

Focus Areas

Access Requirement

Determines whether the environment only needs administrative access or full hybrid network routing.

Traffic & Download Pattern

Reviews upload/download volume, workload traffic, latency sensitivity, and cloud-to-on-premise dependency.

Availability & Resiliency

Assesses whether redundancy, automatic failover, or predictable private connectivity is required.

Operations & Budget

Balances the recommended design against team maturity, operational complexity, and cost sensitivity.
