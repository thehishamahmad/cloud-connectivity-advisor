Overview

Cloud Connectivity Advisor helps organizations evaluate the most suitable connectivity approach before adopting cloud infrastructure and hybrid operations.

The advisor focuses on practical business and infrastructure considerations such as branch connectivity, secure access requirements, cloud traffic expectations, download performance concerns, resiliency needs, operational simplicity, and future scalability.

This tool is designed for organizations that are planning cloud adoption and want a clearer understanding of the connectivity model that best fits their operational and business requirements.

Why This Matters

Avoid Unnecessary Connectivity Cost

Not every organization moving to cloud requires VPN tunnels, dedicated connectivity, or complex hybrid networking architecture. Choosing the wrong model may introduce unnecessary cost and operational effort.

Simplify Early Cloud Adoption

Many organizations are still exploring cloud adoption and may not fully understand which connectivity approach is actually required for their workloads, users, and operations.

Plan User & Branch Access

Cloud adoption often raises concerns around branch access, download speed, application performance, and secure remote access. Early planning helps avoid bottlenecks later.

Match Connectivity to Business Needs

Some workloads only require secure administrative or web application access, while others may require continuous private connectivity, higher resiliency, or predictable bandwidth.
